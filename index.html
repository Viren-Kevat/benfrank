The Coach

Frank Mcchriston.
Federal Fitness 177.

Born in Wilmer, Texas. Raised in the Highland Hills area of Oak Cliff, Dallas. Wilma Hutchins High School, class of 2003. Frank Mcchriston's journey wasn't a straight line it was complicated, costly, and ultimately, completely transforming. After serving nearly 7 years in federal prison, Frank came home with one mission: build something real.

Federal Fitness 177 was born inside those walls. The "177" are the last three digits of Frank's federal inmate ID a number he turned into a movement. Every workout in the system was developed, tested, and proven during years of daily training with zero equipment. Pure calisthenics. Pure discipline. Pure results. Now Frank brings that same intensity and that same faith to every client from 40-year-olds rebuilding their bodies to young men who need direction, not just a workout plan.
